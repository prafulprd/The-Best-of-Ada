Ada Arts District

In the heart of Ada lies a unique arts community full of unique art and creativity. Located downtown on Main Street, the Arts District of Ada includes shops, restaurants, the Hallie Brown Ford Fine Arts Center, a sculpture garden, the Centennial Plaza, historic buildings and numerous events. Among this district's thriving attractions, be sure to see the 27 ft tall iron horse sculpture, paint your own pottery or attend outdoor events in Centennial Plaza.
