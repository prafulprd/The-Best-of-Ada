Sliver Spurr Motel

For a simple slice of rest and relaxation, head inside Silver Spur Motel in Ada. This motel boasts clean rooms and nothing more than a cozy full or queen-size bed, an ice box, a microwave and a flat screen TV. Extended stay guests can whip up quick meals in their personal kitchenettes, too, while enjoying full access to Direct TV channels. With an emphasis on comfort and cleanliness, Silver Spur Motel is the perfect place to rest your head for a night or a week's stay.
