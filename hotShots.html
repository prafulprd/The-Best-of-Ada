Hot Shots Cafe

Swing by Hot Shots Coffee House in Ada for specialty beverages, along with delicious pastries, sandwiches and in-store performances by local musicians. Enjoy a steaming fresh cup of coffee on a chilly fall day, or cool off with cold-brew in the summer months. Infuse your favorite beverage with seasonal flavors like pumpkin spice, or grab a flavored lemonade before you head out to explore historic downtown Ada. (If you're feeling decadent, be sure to try Hot Shots' famous Shelton Swirl, a cheesecake latte generously topped with caramel and chocolate.) Hot Shots also hosts regular open mic events and serves as a venue during the annual AdaFest music festival. Come see what makes this beloved coffee shop near the idyllic campus of East Central University one of Ada's premier hangouts.
