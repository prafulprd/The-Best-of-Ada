The Praire Kitchen

Prairie Kitchen in Ada serves up flavorful breakfast and lunch selections. Stop by this family-owned business for made from scratch pancakes and waffles, as well as a build your own omelette option. This restaurant has a small town feel with friendly waitresses quick to refill low drinks.Stop by for lunch and chow down on buffalo chicken tenders, popcorn shrimp, salads and smoked pork chops.
